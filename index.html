About

Tare is a data tool that maps supply chain exposure. In measurement, tare is the weight of the container — what you subtract to find the true weight of the contents.

Crisis exposure scores are computed from USDA cost-of-production data, FAO food price indices, and country-level Hormuz import dependency estimates from UN Comtrade. Commodity baselines use World Bank Pink Sheet prices for Feb 2026. Exchange rates via Frankfurter (ECB). Updated weekly.
